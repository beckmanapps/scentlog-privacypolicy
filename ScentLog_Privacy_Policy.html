ScentLog Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the ScentLog app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by BeckmanApps (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters: ScentLog is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data entered into the Application is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

All fragrance collection data is stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite database storage. This includes:

  • Fragrance names, houses, and details
  • Notes (top, middle, base), accords, and concentrations
  • Personal ratings and volume information
  • Collection organization preferences
  • App settings and theme preferences
  • Wishlist items

This data remains private and under your control at all times. You can export your data at any time through the app's backup feature.

Third Party Access

The Application does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs that may collect information used to identify you.

Data Sharing

Since no data leaves your device, there is no sharing of your information with any third parties, including:

  • No data sharing with advertisers
  • No data sharing with analytics companies
  • No data sharing with other users
  • No cloud backups to external servers

Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. Since the app stores all data locally and does not transmit information, there is no collection of data from users of any age.

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on the security measures you have in place on your device, including:

  • Device screen locks and passwords
  • Device encryption settings
  • Operating system security updates
  • Physical security of your device

We recommend enabling device security features and keeping your operating system updated for optimal protection.

Data Backup and Export

The Application provides functionality to export your collection data to JSON format. When you use this export feature:

  • You control where the file is saved
  • You control who has access to the exported file
  • The app does not transmit the file anywhere
  • You are responsible for the security of exported files

Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: Required to import/export your collection data (JSON & CSV files)

These permissions are used only for the stated purposes and do not enable any data collection or transmission.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any data within the app
  • Export: You can export your data using the backup feature
  • Deletion: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or using the "Delete All" feature
